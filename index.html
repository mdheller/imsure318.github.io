I obtained my doctoral degree at the University of Stavanger, Norway, in 2019, under the supervision of Prof. Krisztian Balog. I used to be as a short-term visiting scholar at Carnegie Mellon University in 2019, visiting Prof. Jamie Callan. My main research interests include information retrieval and text mining. My Ph.D. research is concerned with developing intelligent search technology for tabular data. Before Ph.D., I obtained two master’s degrees in Computer Science and Telecommunications Engineering from the University of Stavanger and Nanjing Unversity of Posts and Telecommunications, China, respectively.
